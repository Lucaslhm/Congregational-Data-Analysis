Congregational Data Analysis and Visualization:
A Video Explanation

This video, produced by the Ministry Discernment Team, presents an initial broad analysis of our findings. Utilizing open-source data and our web interface, we discuss and demonstrate our insights. While our interpretations of the data are subjective, the data itself remains objective. We are committed to maintaining openness in both our tools and data collection, enabling others to form their own subjective opinions based on the same objective foundation as ours.
